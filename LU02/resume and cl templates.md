Part 5: Always be iterating

&nbsp;	Even with a strong prompt, AI-generated documents may need some refinement. Review the tailored resumes and 

&nbsp;	cover letters carefully to ensure they meet the following criteria:

&nbsp;	Accuracy: All information must be factually correct and match your master resume.

&nbsp;	Clarity and Flow: The language should be clear and easy to read. Check for any awkward phrasing or 

&nbsp;	sentences that don't make sense.

&nbsp;	Keyword Integration: Ensure that keywords from the job posting are incorporated naturally and not just 

&nbsp;	"stuffed" into the text.

&nbsp;	Tone: The tone should be professional and confident, reflecting your personality and enthusiasm.

&nbsp;	Relevance: Every bullet point and sentence should contribute to demonstrating why you are a great fit for 

&nbsp;	this specific job.
