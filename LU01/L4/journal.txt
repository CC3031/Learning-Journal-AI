Prompt Engineering - refining inputs to get optimal AI outputs.

Prompt engineering is powerful, but AI is not perfect even with perfect prompts.

Structure:
1. Task: what do you want to do?
	Persona: Who is the AI? (marketer, teacher)
	Format: Output type (tables, csv file, list)

2. Context:
	Good: "Ideas for a gift under $30."
	Great: "5 ideas for a 29 year-old who loves winter sports and just switched from snowboarding to skiing. Budget: $30."

3. Reference
	Provide additional data, examples, or specific ideas to guide the AI.

4. Evaluate
	Review the output.

5. Iterate
	Feed errors back in.
	Add more details or refine the constraints.


Refinement Strategies
If results are lacking:
1. Mark up prompt into T-C-R
	T: Make a schedule for the team (output: csv)
	C: Specific details about individual schedules
	R: Past schedule templates
2. Separate into shorter tasks
		Don't use monolithic prompts
		Split up into multiple prompts

3. Try different phrasing
	Use an analogous task: instead of "Write a marketing plan," try "Write a story about how this product fits into a customer's life."

4. Introduce Constraints
	"Only music from Canada."
	"Recipes that take less than 30 minutes."
